Door Dasher

Door Dasher is a game made by me and my friend Ayan for Brackey's 2024.1 Game Jam with the theme "What's Behind the Door?". In this game, you will experience the average day of a DoorDash Employee. You must ride your bike to your customer's houses and deliver their pizza orders. However, each person is a little unique and for some reason all of them find an excuse to fight you.
